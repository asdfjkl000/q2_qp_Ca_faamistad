MISSION AND VISION

Autism Speaks envisions a society where individuals on the autism spectrum are fully included and can lead fulfilling lives. Their mission encompasses raising public awareness about autism, fostering understanding and acceptance, and supporting research initiatives to uncover the causes and effective interventions for autism spectrum disorder. They aim to empower individuals with autism and their families by providing resources, education, and advocacy, ultimately striving for a world in which every person with autism has the opportunity to reach their full potential. Autism Speaks collaborates with a wide range of stakeholders, including researchers, policymakers, and the community, to create a more compassionate and supportive environment for individuals living with autism.

WHO ARE WE?

Autism Speaks is an autism advocacy organization that aims to promote solutions for individuals with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) and their families. Our mission typically involves increasing awareness, advocating for research and support, and improving the lives of those affected by autism. We have been involved in various initiatives related to research, awareness campaigns, and support for individuals on the autism spectrum.
