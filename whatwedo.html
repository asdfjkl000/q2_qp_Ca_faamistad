THE KINDNESS CAMPAIGN

The Autism Speaks Kindness Campaign encourages acceptance, understanding and inclusion with daily acts of kindness. You can sign up for The Kindness Campaign anytime of the year; it's also a great way to celebrate World Autism Month in April or World Kindness Day in November.

Celebrate and encourage acceptance, understanding and inclusion with daily acts of kindness.

SOCIAL FUNDRAISING

Once you register for an event, start a fundraiser through your event participant center to have the funds raised count toward your event goal and fundraising prizes. Fundraising through social media in your event Participant Center is one of the fastest and easiest ways to reach your event fundraising goal

You can contact us in our Instagram, Tiktok, and LinkedIn accounts.

You can help support our vision of a world where all people with autism can reach reach their full potential by fundraising or donating on social media.
